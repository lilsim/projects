PASTA

The pasta recipe.

ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomato puree
  • red sauce

instructions

  1. boil the pasta
  2. keep it aside . take a pan and add some oil in it
  3. add some chopped onions and sausages to it
  4. now add the tomato puree to it and let it cook for a minute or 2
  5. finally add pasta to it and serve hot
